CONTACT

OkMultiGun@gmail.com

For questions about registration, divisions, rules, age waivers, or any other event matter, email OkMultiGun@gmail.com. Include your name and the event date in the subject line.

For registration questions, check the following first:

  • Payment confirmation: registration is not complete until PayPal payment is received after PractiScore signup.
  • Payment email: check your spam/junk folder if you don't receive it within a few minutes of registering.
  • Age waivers: competitors under 16 require additional approval — email first.
