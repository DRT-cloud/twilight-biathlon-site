Divisions

Friday — Night Vision Only
NV 2GUN
  • Night Vision Only · White Light Prohibited
  • Rifle: center-fire, 5.45×39 or larger
  • Pistol: 9×19mm or larger
  • NV device required
  • Rifle: 30–50 rds · Pistol: 40–60 rds
  • $100 entry ($50 second division)
Friday — Night Vision Only
NV PCC
  • Night Vision Only · White Light Prohibited
  • PCC: pistol caliber carbine (primary)
  • Pistol optional
  • NV device required
  • PCC: 70–110 rds
  • $100 entry ($50 second division)
Saturday — White Light
2GUN
  • White Light · Headlamps & Weapon Lights Permitted
  • Rifle: center-fire, 5.45×39 or larger
  • Pistol: 9×19mm or larger
  • Rifle: 30–50 rds · Pistol: 40–60 rds
  • $100 entry ($50 second division)
Saturday — White Light
PCC
  • White Light · Headlamps & Weapon Lights Permitted
  • PCC: pistol caliber carbine (primary)
  • Pistol optional
  • PCC: 70–110 rds
  • $100 entry ($50 second division)

All firearms semi-automatic only. Maximum 2 divisions per competitor.
