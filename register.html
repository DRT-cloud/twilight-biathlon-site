How Registration Works

  1. Register on PractiScore using the links above. Select your division(s).
  2. Check your email for PayPal payment details. Check spam/junk if it doesn’t arrive within a few minutes.
  3. Complete PayPal payment. Registration is NOT complete until PayPal payment is received.
  4. Confirmation. You’ll receive a confirmation once payment is processed.

Pricing

  • $100 per division (first entry)
  • $50 second division (same competitor, max 2 divisions)
  • You may compete in one Friday NV division and one Saturday white-light division

Refund Policy

  • Full refund (minus fees) until 60 days before the event
  • Half refund (minus fees) until 30 days before the event
  • No refund within 30 days of the event

Age Requirements

Competitors under 16 require additional approval. Email OkMultiGun@gmail.com before registering.

Questions?

Email OkMultiGun@gmail.com. Include your name and event date in the subject line.
